INFO

No exclusivity, No BS. Creating an approachable and diverse environment for Data Science exploration at USC.

DataSC fosters an environment where individuals across subjects and experience levels learn Data Science and collaboratively engage in cross-disciplinary topics. From ground-up curriculum to hands-on projects, industry speaker sessions with leaders from NVIDIA, Microsoft, Google, and Meta, to career support and genuinely fun social events. Whether you're a complete beginner or experienced practitioner, there's a place for you in our community of data enthusiasts.

Ways to Participate
1
Curriculum
No experience required
Complete beginner-friendly courses covering setup, theory, and application. Our ground-up curriculum walks you through everything from basic concepts to practical implementations, regardless of your background.
2
Join a Project Team
Data science and programming experience required
Apply your knowledge in real-world projects as either a Project Lead or Project Member. Work on cross-disciplinary applications and bridge the gap between learning and doing.
3
Join a Kaggle Competition Team
Interview required
Compete in prestigious data science competitions with fellow club members. Gain experience with real datasets, advanced techniques, and collaborative problem-solving in a competitive environment.

How to Join:

  1. Fill out our quick application.
  2. Select a 15 minute time slot to meet our team.
  3. Congratulations!
